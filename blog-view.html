6 Most Important Money Lessons to Teach your Children

Posted by Author's Name


Last Friday, whilst I was getting ready to head to the office, my little three year old came up to me in tears. She couldn’t understand why I had to leave her and go to work. She wanted me to take her to the airport so that we could go on another holiday (a few months back we visited New Zealand, and she has been dying for the next holiday).

I lied and told her that Daddy has to go to work and earn money to buy her a present. I think she was more eager than my wife to see me off; she even packed my lunch which consisted of her half eaten apple and her princess biscuits. They are so cute at this age.When I got home you can imagine her reaction when I didn’t bring her a present. I had forgotten all about my little porkie in the morning but kids have photographic memories when there are lollies or presents involved. Let’s just say she brought a new meaning to the terrible threes.
